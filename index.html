Source Code Audits

About

Coinspect senior consultants are professionals with more than 20 years of information security experience and an average of 6 years working in cryptocurrency security. Coinspect has contributed to secure key technologies and services of the cryptocurrency ecosystem by auditing, reporting vulnerabilities, and proposing improvements for new cryptocurrency implementations, smart contracts, dapps, mobile apps, hardware wallets, smart contract compilers, DeFi systems, and exchanges.
